Latvia

I was born in Latvia in a small town of Salaspils. It's a shame to say that I never visited after I moved from there. I always wanted to revisit but haven't had a chance yet. So here I will talk about several places that I remember visiting as a kid. Latvia is a lively little European country these days. But even during the soviet times it was considered a "little piece of Europe" in the soviet reality. We had better made furniture, cute cafes and luxury to walk through the medieval streets of Riga, the capital.

Riga has a lot to offer. It has a charming downtown, river in the middle, it's a port city with access to the Baltic sea. From my childhood memories I recommend Riga's Zoo, Mezaparks, Central Market for foodies. Boat rides in the Gulf of Riga are awesome, too.
