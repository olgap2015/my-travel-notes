Sicily

I lived in Sicily for only about 6 months. I've been to a few places in Italy - like Rome, Naples, Sorrento. But I happened to actually live in a small coastal town in Sicily - Oliveri. It's located on the North shore near Messina. Me and my husband got married there and started our life together. My father-in-law was born and spent his young years in Sicily. For this reason my husband was visiting Sicily a lot. Italy is such a famous travel destination these days that I can hardly be original here.

Sicily is paradise on Earth. In my opinion this is the place where you have to rent a car and just drive, drive, drive. Here are some photos I took around Oliveri when I lived there.
